Quick Start

  1. Sign In with your email and password
  2. Set a Start Location at the top center (e.g., "Sydney")
  3. Click Places on the Map to add them to your itinerary
  4. Click "Update Route" to see your driving route
  5. Mark Places as Visited in your Favorites to track your journey
